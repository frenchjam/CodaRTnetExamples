RTNetClient::stopAcq
RTNetClient
Stop a continuous or continuous buffered acquistion.
void stopAcq() throw(NetworkException, DeviceStatusArray);
Description
If the system is currently acquiring data (following a call to startAcqContinuous or startAcqContinuousBuffered), this will put it back into a prepared but non-acquiring state.
Exceptions
NetworkException, DeviceStatusArray
